# v1.6.0 — Fictional market career sim, Market Saga, and release metadata

Release date: 2026-05-06

This release covers the work merged after `v1.5.7` through the `v1.6.0` tag.
It moves the project from a compact stock simulator toward a fuller fictional-market
career sim: cloud saves, clearer trade feedback, advanced portfolio tools, market
depth, fund careers, trophies, and the first Market Saga legacy loop.

## Highlights

- Added Supabase-backed anonymous cloud saves with local IndexedDB fallback.
- Added Phase 1/Phase 2 gameplay surfacing: market regimes, advisor missions,
  company traits, macro backdrop, scanner signals, and stock research briefs.
- Added frictionless trade feedback: previews, confirmations, clearer failed-trade
  messages, long-only guardrails, simplified trading language, and an opportunity board.
- Added portfolio-vs-benchmark performance charts, open-order surfaces, advanced
  conditional orders, and one-action portfolio rebalancing.
- Added market-depth systems: watchlists, scheduled catalysts, market pulse panels,
  upcoming catalyst context, and richer season recaps.
- Added fund career and season-continuation systems with archetypes, rival funds,
  quarterly board reviews, board objectives, and next-season continuity.
- Added the trophy collection and trophy room for long-term goals beyond net worth.
- Added Market Saga legacy story mode Phase 1, including legacy epilogues and
  deterministic sequel chapter choices after completed runs.
- Added CI, README badges, issue/PR templates, branch-protection recommendations,
  `AGENTS.md`, and a project roadmap reference.
- Versioned the save schema so old saves migrate cleanly and newer unsupported saves
  are rejected instead of loading partially.

## User-Facing Changes

### Cloud Saves

- Saves locally first, then syncs to Supabase when `VITE_SUPABASE_URL` and
  `VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY` are configured.
- Loads cloud saves first when available, then falls back to local saves.
- Preserves local-only behavior when Supabase environment variables are missing.

### Trading Flow

- Stock detail now shows live trade previews before buy/sell/short/cover actions.
- Successful trades now show confirmation details: cash impact, position impact,
  exposure, and risk movement.
- Failed trades now explain why the action failed instead of feeling inert.
- Quantity/action changes clear stale confirmations and errors.
- Rapid repeated taps are guarded to reduce accidental duplicate execution.
- Trading language and guardrails are simpler, with clearer long-only defaults.

### Portfolio Tools

- Portfolio now includes normalized net worth vs benchmark chart views.
- Portfolio now surfaces open orders and protective exits.
- Rebalancing accepts stock or sector target weights, previews required trades,
  auto-balances cash, and executes in one action.
- Stock detail supports limit orders, stop-losses, and take-profit exits.

### Market Depth

- Watchlists surface large moves, fresh news, and upcoming catalysts.
- Scheduled catalysts make future events more legible before they resolve.
- Market Pulse, Upcoming Catalysts, and Season Recap panels explain each run's
  sector leadership, breadth, benchmark performance, and key events.
- The market universe now uses 96 fictional stocks with fictional tickers across
  the sector set.

### Career And Legacy

- Fund archetypes, rival funds, quarterly reviews, and board objectives add
  longer-run drama.
- Trophy collection gives players persistent goals outside raw net worth.
- Completed runs generate legacy epilogues and sequel chapter choices that shape
  the next career season.
- Completed runs record automatically to the leaderboard with stable run IDs.
- Final turns route through the turn summary and into Game Over.
- Completed auto-save slots are cleared, and completed manual saves reopen as results.
